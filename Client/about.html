Andrew Kroll

Hey! I’m Andrew Kroll and I have been a professional photographer for a little over a year now, but I have been taking pictures since I was 16. I am working on my Bachelor’s degree in Integrated Strategic Communications with an emphasis on Advertising, and I am minoring in Multimedia. I hope to eventually become a freelance photographer, and co-owning an Advertising Firm.

We have been lucky enough to work with a number of the University of Wisconsin Eau Claire’s choirs and ensembles such as, The Singing Statesmen, the universities tenor bass acapella group, The Innocent Men, and their SATB group, Impromptu Acapella. I have been doing a bunch of freelance work for The Growler Guys, an tap house in Eau Claire. I also did a shoot for an engagement, and we will both be shooting a wedding during the winter of 2023.

I am primarily a portrait photographer, both candid and posed. Producing a picture that I am proud of sharing, and one that my client is proud to be apart of is incredibly important to me and my style.

I hope you decide to work with us!
